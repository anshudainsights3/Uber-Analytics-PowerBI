# Uber-Analytics-PowerBI  
**Project Title:** Uber Performance & Operations Dashboard  

An interactive **Power BI Dashboard** designed to analyze Uber’s operational and business performance metrics — including completed rides, revenue, driver ratings, cancellations, and vehicle-type analysis — providing valuable insights for strategic decision-making and efficiency improvement.

---

## Project Description
The **Uber Analytics Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of Uber’s ride-hailing performance. It tracks and visualizes booking data, revenue trends, and customer engagement, helping stakeholders identify key operational patterns and optimize service delivery.  

The dashboard enables users to:  
- Monitor **completed, incomplete, and cancelled rides** in real time.  
- Evaluate **revenue growth** by vehicle type (Auto, Go Mini, Go Sedan, Premier Sedan, Uber-X).  
- Analyze **driver and customer ratings** to measure service satisfaction.  
- Identify **top pickup and drop locations** for demand forecasting.  

---

## Tech Stack
The dashboard was developed using:  
1. **Power BI Desktop** – for creating visual analytics and interactivity.  
2. **Power Query** – for data extraction, transformation, and cleaning.  
3. **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – for KPI measures (Revenue, Distance, Bookings).  
4. **Data Modeling** – to connect tables (Rides, Drivers, Customers, Vehicles).  
5. **File Format:** `.pbix` for dashboard development and `.png` for visualization preview.  

---

## Data Source
The dataset simulates **Uber’s ride and revenue data**, including:  
- Ride details: **Completed, Incomplete, and Cancelled bookings**.  
- Financial data: **Revenue by vehicle type** and monthly trends.  
- Ratings: **Driver and Customer average ratings**.  
- Distance covered and operational metrics.  

---

## Features / Highlights
**Business Problem:**  
Uber needs to analyze its operational efficiency, revenue generation, and customer experience across different vehicle types and cities.  

**Goal of the Dashboard:**  
To provide a unified analytical view of Uber’s ride performance, enabling decision-makers to optimize operations, reduce cancellations, and improve driver-customer satisfaction.  

---

## Key Questions Answered
- How many **rides were completed, incomplete, or cancelled** this month?  
- Which **vehicle type generates the highest revenue**?  
- What are the **top pickup and drop locations**?  
- What is the **average driver and customer rating**?  
- How do **monthly revenue and booking trends** fluctuate throughout the year?  

---

## Key Visuals
- **KPI Tiles:** Completed Bookings, Lost Bookings, Total Revenue, Total Distance.  
- **Line Chart:** Monthly trend of completed bookings.  
- **Bar Chart:** Revenue by vehicle type.  
- **Donut Charts:** Ride completion distribution (Completed, Incomplete, Cancelled).  
- **Card Visuals:** Top Pickup (Khandsa) & Top Drop (Ashram).  
- **Rating Section:** Average Driver Rating and Average Customer Rating.  

---

## Business Impact & Insights
- **Operational Overview:** 93K completed bookings with 57K lost bookings tracked.  
- **Revenue Performance:** Generated 52M total revenue across 2.51M km distance.  
- **Customer Insights:** Average customer satisfaction rating of 4.4.  
- **Driver Insights:** Average driver performance rating of 4.23.  
- **Vehicle Analysis:** Auto and Go Mini vehicles show the highest revenue share.
